## What will My customers see?

With Beans, you have full control over what your customers experience. The Beans API allows
you to customize the entire loyalty experience to match your brand. Your customers will see
exactly what you want them to see.

### Setting up and customization

Beans' API and plugins enable seamless integration into your website. You control the design
and styling, ensuring that the loyalty experience is tailored to your brand and fully aligns with your site's look and feel.
