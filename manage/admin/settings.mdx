The Settings section of your Beans dashboard allows you to configure shared settings for all Beans apps.
You can view and update your store details, edit your personal information, and manage access to your Beans workspace.
The plan and billing options also enable you to upgrade your plan and test your program before launching it.

## General

<Frame>
  <img src="/assets/images/admin/admin-settings/general.webp" alt="general" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

This section allows you to view and update key store details, including branding and style elements like your logo and
primary store color, along with your store name, website URL, language, currency, category, and region.

- Here is the [list of supported currencies.](https://www.trybeans.com/i18n)

- Here is the [list of supported languages.](https://www.trybeans.com/i18n)

## Profile

<Frame>
  <img src="/assets/images/admin/admin-settings/profile.webp" alt="profile" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

In this section, you can edit your personal information, including your email address, first name, and last name.
