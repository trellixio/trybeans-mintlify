## FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Beans

### Who Is Beans For?

Beans is designed for e-commerce businesses. Whether you sell physical goods or offer services
online, Beans can be utilized to support your operations.

### How to Get Started with Beans

To use Beans without programming, check if an integration is available for your e-commerce
platform. If you plan to work directly with the programming interface, refer to the API
documentation for implementation details.

### Phone Support

Do You Provide It?

No, we don’t offer phone support—but please don't take this as a sign of
disinterest! We're eager to help and answer your questions.

While it might seem unusual, we've found that beginning with email allows us to provide the
quickest and most effective response. Our goal is to deliver fast, high-quality answers via
email, ensuring you're always talking to a human that can help first-hand. If a phone call
is needed after that, we're happy to arrange one.

### Why is Beans better than other options?

Here are some reasons why others have chosen Beans:

**End-user experience focus**: Beans is one of the few customer reward programs that prioritizes
user engagement. Every part of our infrastructure is built with the end user in mind, ensuring
that your customers stay engaged.

**Managed and hassle-free**: Beans takes care of everything—from balance reminders to activity
notifications—so your customers won't forget about you, allowing you to focus on growing your
business.

**Flexible reward rules**: Beans offers an extensive range of customizable rules that let you
reward customers for nearly every action that matters to your business.

**User-friendly interface**: The interface is incredibly simple and easy to navigate.

**Powerful API**: Beans comes with an elegant and robust API, making it quick and easy to
integrate into your website, ensuring a seamless experience.

**No coding required**: Several plugins are available, enabling you to get started in minutes
without any coding skills.

**No legacy systems**: Beans offers a modern, user-friendly alternative to outdated loyalty
programs, giving you complete control over the reward experience.

**Transparent pricing**: We offer both a free and a premium plan, with no hidden fees or limits
on the points you can issue or redeem. Our pricing is easy to understand, with no surprises.

**Excellent support**: When you need assistance, you'll receive fast, personalized help from real
people.

### What will my customers see?

With Beans, you have full control over what your customers experience. The Beans API allows
you to customize the entire loyalty experience to match your brand. Your customers will see
exactly what you want them to see.

### Technical support

**Can I reach an engineer to talk about technical issues?**

Yes, you can reach out to an engineer for technical issues. Our support team includes
engineers who are well-equipped to assist with any technical questions. If the first point
of contact doesn’t have the answer, they will find the right person to help.

Feel free to email us anytime. We aim to respond as quickly as possible, typically within
48 hours.

### Security

We adhere to industry standards, ensuring that every part of our infrastructure and each
stage of our operations are built with security in mind. Beans uses HTTPS across all
services, including our public website. We routinely audit our security measures,
including the certificates we provide, the certificate authorities we rely on, and the
ciphers we support.
