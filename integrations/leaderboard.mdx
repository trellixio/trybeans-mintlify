The Leaderboard adds a layer of gamification to your rewards program by allowing
members to track and compare their performance with others in a friendly, engaging environment.
[This Beans integration](https://www.trybeans.com/integrations/trellis:leaderboard) highlights
your most active members over a specific period, offering recognition and motivation without
compromising user privacy.

## Setup

To connect to the Leaderboard integration:

1. From the Beans admin page, navigate to **Integrations**.
2. Locate and select **Leaderboard ** from the available integrations.
3. Click **Connect** to enable the integration.

## How It Works

Once activated, customers can join the Leaderboard to compete with others, earn more points,
and climb to the top of your rewards program rankings.

To join the Leaderboard:

1. From the rewards program page on your store, navigate to the **Leaderboard** section.
2. Click the **Join the Leaderboard** button.
3. Enter a pseudonym and click **Save**.

## Leaderboard log

The Leaderboard Log provides a clear snapshot of member engagement
within the Beans rewards system, ranking participants based on the total points
they've earned over a set period. Merchants can quickly identify top-performing
shoppers and monitor progress.
