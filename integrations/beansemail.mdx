Beans Email,is a resource to manage campaigns and promote rewards program updates via email,
enhancing customer engagement. This integration helps merchants to communicate directly with
customers, ensuring they stay informed about new opportunities to earn and redeem loyalty
points.

## Setup

1. From the Beans admin page, go to **Integrations**.

2. Locate **Beans email** from the list of available integrations.

3. Click on the **Connect** button and proceed as directed.

## Emails Log

This interface provides a full record of all emails sent through Beans, along
with in-depth insights into email interactions such as:

1. Email send date.

2. The event that triggers the email.

3. Email recipient.

4. Status of email delivery (opened, delivered, etc.).
