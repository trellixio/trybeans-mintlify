Unlock the power of personalized customer engagement with Beans Emails, a built-in email marketing solution within the
Beans loyalty platform. [Integrating Beans with Beans email](https://www.trybeans.com/integrations/trellis:foxx) empowers businesses to connect directly with customers through targeted, tailored communications. By seamlessly merging Beans Emails with the loyalty program, merchants can effortlessly promote rewards, updates, and loyalty opportunities via email, engaging a more informed, enthusiastic, and engaged customer base.

## Setup

1. From the Beans admin page, go to integration.

2. Locate Beans email from the list of available integrations.

3. Click on the **connect** button and proceed as directed.

## Emails Log

The email log section provides a full record of all emails sent through Beans, along with in-depth insights into email interactions such as:

1. Email send date.

2. The event that triggers the email.

3. Email recipient.

4. Status of email delivery (opened, delivered, etc.).
