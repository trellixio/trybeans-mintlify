WooCommerce is a flexible and customizable e-commerce platform that allows merchants to create and manage their online store. It's open-source, and tailored to enhance your specific business needs.

## Setting Up Reward and Referral Programs with Beans

Before proceeding with WooCommerce installation, it's crucial to configure your reward and referral programs using the Beans plugin. These programs are instrumental in motivating customers and boosting sales. Follow these steps to create and set up these programs:

### Create a Reward program:

- On the Beans plugin page, navigate to the “Go to Reward page” to enable the reward program page.

- Configure the [rewards settings](https://app.trybeans.com/$realverse/liana/rules), including the rewards customers can earn.

- Configure the[ referral settings](https://app.trybeans.com/$realverse/bamboo/rules), including the rewards for successful referrals

## Manually installing Beans

This article provides a guide on manually connecting Beans to your WooCommerce store.

You may encounter difficulties setting up Beans on your WooCommerce due to server configuration. In such cases, you can attempt a manual connection by following the steps below.

🛈 Before you start, ensure you are logged into both your Beans admin dashboard and WordPress admin dashboard.

Steps

1. Go to WooCommerce > Settings > Advanced > REST API and click the Add key.

2. Generate the API key and select permissions.

3. Go to the [manual installation page](https://connect.trybeans.com/radix/woocommerce/setup/) and paste the consumer secret and consumer key into the displayed form.

4. Click on “Save” to complete the setup
