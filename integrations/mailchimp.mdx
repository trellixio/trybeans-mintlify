MailChimp is an email marketing platform that supports creating effective email campaigns to
engage audiences. [Integrating Beans with MailChimp](https://www.trybeans.com/integrations/trellis:mailchimp)
enables merchants to embed loyalty rewards in email communications, increasing customer
engagement and encouraging repeat purchases through targeted, reward-focused emails.

## Setup

1. From the Beans admin, go to **Integrations**.
2. Locate **MailChimp** in the list of available integrations.
3. Click on the **Connect** button and proceed as directed.
4. Log in to your existing account, If you don't have an account, sign up for MailChimp.
5. Grant Beans permission to interact with your Mailchimp account.

## Status

This interface provides control over the status and data synchronization between Beans
and MailChimp, offering options to disconnect the integration, or reset the connection
as needed.

To access the status page:

1. Navigate to the MailChimp integration home page
2. Click on **Status** in the integration navigation menu.
