[MailChimp](https://mailchimp.com/), a top email marketing platform, plays a vital role in creating impactful email campaigns that effectively connect with
your audience. [Integrating Beans with MailChimp](https://www.trybeans.com/integrations/trellis:mailchimp) elevate your marketing efforts by seamlessly including
loyalty rewards within your email communications. This integration helps merchants boost customer engagement by incentivizing interactions and
driving repeat purchases through targeted, reward-driven emails.

## Setup

1. From the Beans admin, go to **Integrations**.
2. Locate **MailChimp** in the list of available integrations.
3. Click on the **Connect** button and proceed as directed.
4. Log in to your existing account, If you don't have an account, sign up for MailChimp.
5. Grant Beans permission to interact with your Mailchimp account.

## Status

This interface provides control over the status and data synchronization between Beans and MailChimp, offering options to disconnect
the integration, or reset the connection as needed.

To access the status page:

1. Navigate to the MailChimp integration home page
2. Click on **Status** in the integration navigation menu.
